## type ##

**Type:** `string`

**Value:** `<type-str>`

**Attribute:** required

**Parent:** [struct](struct.md)

To specify the type of C++ object. **Duplicated types are not allowed** .

Recommended name-style:

* all lowercase
* with underscores between word
* with a trailing `_t`
