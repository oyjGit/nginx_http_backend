## types ##

The built-in types of slothjson include the following:

* `bool`
* `int8_t`
* `uint8_t`
* `int16_t`
* `uint16_t`
* `int32_t`
* `uint32_t`
* `int64_t`
* `uint64_t`
* `float`
* `double`
* `string`

`string` will be parsed as `std::string` by `slothjson.py`.
